# ⚛️ GFL Track C: Advanced Energy Frontiers

## Overview
Track C represents the expansion phase of the Grandfather Legacy (GFL) subnet. While Tracks A and B focus on Lattice QED and Hydrogen-Loading simulations, Track C leverages the decentralized compute power of the Bittensor network to model high-energy physics transitions.

## Research Areas
- **Fission/Fusion Dynamics:** Simulating neutron flux and plasma stability in localized environments.
- **Zero-Point Energy (ZPE) Fluctuations:** Modeling vacuum energy density and potential extraction pathways.
- **Phonon-Assisted Nuclear Reactions:** Expanding the Track B Pd-H model to include resonant frequency interactions.

## Incentive Logic (The Entropy Bounty)
Track C introduces a new reward tier for miners who provide high-fidelity data in unstable simulation environments:
- **Accuracy Multiplier:** Rewards based on the Delta E ($ΔE$) precision relative to known experimental benchmarks.
- **Computational Depth:** Incentives for multi-physics coupling (Thermal + Chemical + Quantum).
